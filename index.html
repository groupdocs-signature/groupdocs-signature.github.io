GroupDocs

Signature

Product Family

GroupDocs.Signature is a electronic signature API's for .NET and Java that supports over 50 document & image formats and over 6 different signature types. eSign documents with electronic signature (certificate), image, stamp, text, hand written signature, qrCode or barCode, search and validate signatures. Add Custom Data Object, VCard or Email Object to QR-Code & Verify Encrypted QR-Code in PDF Files and much more!
